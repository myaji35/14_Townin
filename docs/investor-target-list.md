**Goal:** Build relationships before formal pitch

**Actions:**
1. **LinkedIn Engagement:**
   - Connect with partners at target VCs
   - Comment thoughtfully on their posts (AI, insurtech topics)
   - Share Townin-related content (e.g., "Korea's aging crisis = care tech opportunity")

2. **Mutual Connections:**
   - Map network (2nd-degree connections to VCs)
   - Ask for intros: "I'm building [one-liner], would you intro me to [VC name]?"

3. **VC Events:**
   - Attend Primer Camp info session

   - Startup Alliance networking events

### Phase 3: Pitch Meetings (Month 2)
**Goal:** Deliver compelling pitch, get term sheets

**Meeting Structure (20 minutes):**
1. **Hook (2 min):** Problem statement (3 massive problems)
2. **Solution (3 min):** 3-layer platform demo (show Flyer AI working)
3. **Market (2 min):** $12B TAM, perfect timing
4. **Traction (3 min):** Validation results, bowling pin plan
5. **Team (2 min):** Your background, advisor names
6. **Ask (3 min):** $300K pre-seed, terms, timeline
7. **Q&A (5 min)**

**Follow-Up:**
- Send deck within 2 hours (while you're fresh in their mind)
- Include validation results data (flyer AI accuracy report, GraphRAG demo video)
- Follow up weekly if no response

---

### Phase 4: Diligence & Close (Month 3)
**Goal:** Close $300K within 90 days of first outreach

**Diligence Prep:**
- **Data Room:** Google Drive with all docs (PRD, tech specs, market research, financials)
- **Reference Calls:** Line up 3-5 advisors/experts who can vouch for you
- **Technical Deep Dive:** Be ready for CTO-level grilling on GraphRAG architecture
- **Legal:** Use standard safe note template (no need for custom terms at Pre-Seed)

**Negotiation Points:**
- **Valuation:** Target $2M post-money (15% dilution for $300K)
- **Safe Note:** 20% discount + $10M cap (standard for Pre-Seed)
- **Pro-Rata Rights:** Give investors right to participate in Seed round
- **Board Seats:** None at Pre-Seed (wait until Series A)
